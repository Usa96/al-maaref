Dubai School Inspection Bureau

The Dubai School Inspection Bureau (DSIB) of KHDA was established by Decision 38 of The Executive Council of the Government of Dubai in 2007. DSIB provides comprehensive information on the standard of private school education in Dubai, helping to inform improvement planning at both school and policy levels. This is achieved through various approaches, including annual school inspections, parents' meetings and focus groups, and surveys of teachers, parents, and students.

Through inspections and the participation of schools in international assessments, DSIB provides KHDA, the Government of Dubai, and the wider public with an accurate and detailed view of the quality of school education in Dubai, compared to the best-performing countries in the world.
