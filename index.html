Welcome to Al Maaref Private School

We’re on a mission to equip every leader, dreamer and inbetweener with the inner confidence, self belief and personal resilience to thrive in the classroom and beyond. With small class sizes, an unparalleled breadth of activities for pupils, global alumni and a rich heritage of over 500 years – Al Maaref is a uniquely different and exceptional environment for each and every pupil.

Al Maaref is designed for boys and girls aged from 3 to 18. Our school currently welcomes students from FS1 to Year 9, and we are accepting applications for Year 10 for a September 2025 start. The school will grow year by year in line with KHDA regulations.

Our school embodies the ethos and values that have underpinned the Al Maaref Private School for over 500 years. These, combined with a spirit of innovation and development, will prepare pupils for the challenges of the 21st and 22nd centuries. We lead by example, stay true to our values, innovate, collaborate and strive to be the very best we can be.

A Community of Excellence and Inclusion

Al Maaref Private School is a kind, inclusive, tolerant community where diverse opportunity, personal development, high achievement and collaborative partnership define who we are, and where our values underpin all we do.

Respect

We nurture an atmosphere of mutual respect between all members of our community, fostering self-esteem, emotional maturity and lasting friendships in a positive, happy environment.

Scholarship

We encourage the growth of intellectual curiosity, independence and innovation within a scholarly environment supported by specialists who inspire lifelong learning.

Inclusivity

We remain loyal to our founding principles to educate students from all backgrounds, fostering diversity, acceptance, and a deep sense of belonging.

Integrity

We promote self-discipline, responsibility and uphold values like honesty, humility, and kindness.

Courage

We help students embrace challenges, take risks, and persevere with resilience and confidence.

Collaboration

We instill a spirit of cooperation, teamwork and mutual respect to make a difference in our community and world.
