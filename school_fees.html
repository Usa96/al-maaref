Understanding Our Fees and Payment Policies

Tuition Fees and Payment Schedule

Fees are payable annually or term-wise (40%-30%-30%). Excludes optional activities or services.

Deposit

New students must pay a non-refundable 10% deposit of annual tuition.

Application Fee

AED 500 non-refundable fee per student.

Refund Policy

Refunds follow KHDA rules. Some conditions apply.
