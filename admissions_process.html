Take the first step towards enrolling your child at AMPS

Applications for admission are accepted at all times during the school year, although there are cut-off dates during the year, which are designated by the UAE Ministry of Education.

Applications must be completed, and the required non-refundable application fee progressed. If there are no places available at the time, then the application is placed on the school’s waiting list.

When a place becomes available, the school will contact the first eligible applicant and invite them to attend an assessment.

Make an Enquiry

The admissions process is a simple journey from initial enquiry to first day at school:

  1. Make an enquiry and have a discussion with our friendly Admissions Team.
  2. Visit us to get a feel for our school and meet our exceptional School Leadership Team.
  3. Apply for a place by completing the online application form.
  4. We will arrange for an assessment. The assessment will help staff ensure they understand the individual learning needs of each pupil.
  5. We look forward to welcoming our new pupils into the AMPS family.

Fee Policy and Payment Instructions

Admission Fee: AED 500 (non-refundable) is payable at the time of new registration. AED 250 will be charged on all bounced cheques.

For New Parents: A 10% deposit of the annual tuition fee must be paid within 7 days of receiving an offer of a place. This is non-refundable unless withdrawn per our Withdrawal Policy.

For Current Parents: A 5% deposit of the annual tuition fee or AED 500 (whichever is higher) is due at re-registration. Non-refundable unless withdrawn as per policy.

Remaining Payment: Payment is to be made via cheques – three post-dated cheques must be submitted upon acceptance or re-registration.

  • Books, uniform, transport and other fees are to be paid by cash or with the first installment cheque.
  • 1st Installment: 40% by cash/cheque dated 25th August 2024
  • 2nd Installment: 30% by cheque dated 7th December 2024
  • 3rd Installment: 30% by cheque dated 7th March 2025

All fees must be paid before the first day of school to ensure access to campus facilities.
